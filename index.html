Welcome to Trivia With Tyson

Trivia With Tyson is an exciting project that brings you interesting trivia questions on various topics. Our goal is to make learning fun and engaging by providing curated trivia videos on YouTube.

Our app allows users to generate trivia videos effortlessly. Simply select a category and the number of questions you want to include, and our app will handle the rest.

Features:

  • Curated trivia questions on various topics
  • Easy-to-use interface for generating trivia videos
  • Automatic upload to your YouTube channel
